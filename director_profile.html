Nikhil Premraj

I am a filmmaker from Kerala with a deep passion for storytelling and visual craftsmanship. With over 15 years in the media industry, I started in television production, creating content for platforms like Rose Bowl TV, Kairali TV, and Asianet.

Over the years, I expanded my expertise into filmmaking, advertising, and corporate video production. As an assistant director, I contributed to seven films, refining my craft under industry veterans.

I later worked as a chief associate director on three films, including the critically acclaimed Kuttanpillayude Shivarathri (2018) in 2022. I made my directorial debut with Aanapparambile World Cup, a sports drama starring Antony Varghese Pepe.

The film received widespread appreciation, with a theatrical release on November 25, 2022, followed by OTT streaming on Sunnext.

Beyond cinema, I specialize in corporate video production and ad filmmaking, crafting compelling visual narratives for brands, businesses, and organizations. My company, Mood, has been delivering high-quality commercials, social media content, and digital marketing solutions across India since 2018.

With over 50 completed commercials spanning education, hospitality, and various other sectors, I strive to create content that resonates and engages.

Looking forward to collaborating and bringing impactful stories to life. Let's connect!
